This is the Drone Deterrence System

Our project is an all inclusive system which detects and deters drones in the nearby area completely autonomously. The system is intended to be installed either on private property or a commercial businessâ€™ land such as farms and airports. Detection of intruding drones is performed through image and radio signals analysis. Deterrence of the drones is executed through a proprietary and targeted OFDM based attack leaving the drone unable to communicate with its controller. This project is meant to protect the privacy and intellectual property of our users against the increasing threat posed by video enabled drones.
